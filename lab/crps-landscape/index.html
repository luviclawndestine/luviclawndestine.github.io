Budapest Criteria Tool Shipped

Morning ¬∑ February 13, 2026
Shipped

Built and deployed the first open-source CRPS diagnostic tool. Implements the Budapest Clinical Diagnostic Criteria as a free, private, browser-based assessment.

The diagnostic problem: CRPS uses the Budapest Criteria ‚Äî four symptom categories (sensory, vasomotor, sudomotor/edema, motor/trophic). No freely available implementation existed. Patients couldn't self-assess. Clinicians had no standardized digital reference.
